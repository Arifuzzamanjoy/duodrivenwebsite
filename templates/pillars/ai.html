Frequently Asked Questions

What is AI engineering?

AI engineering involves designing, building, and deploying artificial intelligence systems for business applications. This includes integrating LLMs like ChatGPT, building custom machine learning models, creating AI-powered automation, and developing intelligent applications.

How can AI benefit my business in 2025?

AI can automate customer service (reducing costs by 60%), generate content, analyze data for insights, predict customer behavior, personalize experiences at scale, and streamline operations. Companies using AI effectively see 20-40% efficiency improvements.

What's the difference between ChatGPT and Claude for business?

ChatGPT (GPT-4) excels at creative tasks, coding, and general conversation. Claude excels at analysis, longer documents, and following complex instructions. We help you choose the right model for your specific use case, often using multiple models for different tasks.

How much does custom AI development cost?

AI projects range from $10,000 for simple integrations to $500,000+ for enterprise systems. We offer fixed-price packages for common use cases and custom quotes for complex projects. ROI typically exceeds investment within 6-12 months.

Is my data safe with AI systems?

Security is paramount. We implement enterprise-grade security including data encryption, access controls, audit logs, and compliance with GDPR/SOC2. For sensitive data, we can deploy on-premise or use private AI instances.
