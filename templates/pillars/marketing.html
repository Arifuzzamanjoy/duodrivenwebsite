How long does SEO take to show results?

SEO typically shows initial improvements within 3-6 months, with significant results in 6-12 months. Factors affecting timeline include website age, competition, content quality, and technical foundation. We provide monthly reports to track progress.

What's the difference between SEO and PPC?

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) focuses on organic rankings and takes time to build but provides long-term traffic. PPC (Pay-Per-Click) provides immediate visibility through paid ads. We recommend a combined approach for optimal results.
